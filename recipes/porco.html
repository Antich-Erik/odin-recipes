Piccolo's Pasta

The perfect mounds of tomato-ey spaghetti from Porco Rosso

Simple and fast dish to enjoy with your loved one, a flight straight to fall in love with pasta.

Wine is optional but of course encouraged… you know, in the name of authenticity.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • 1 Can of crushed tomatoes
  • ¼ Large white onion diced
  • 2-3 Garlic cloves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2-3 Bay leafs
  • ½ Spoon italian spices
  • ½ Spoon or less of sugar
  • ½ Lb of spaghetti

How to cook it?

  1. Heat up the oil and add the onions. Cook in med low until they are slightly soft and slightly see through not all the way. about 3-5 mins
  2. Add the garlic and cook on low for another 3 minutes till everything is soft and slightly colored. Dont cook it till it’s brown then it’ll be burnt and wont taste good
  3. Open the can of crushed tomatoes and add it. Cook on med low/for about 10 mins.
  4. Add the bay leafs, italian spices, salt, and pepper and if you have, some fresh basil. Stir everything and let it cook for another 10-15 minutes. Add more spices if you need it.
  5. Add the sugar, then cook for another 5 minutes, turn the heat off and let it stand.
  6. While the sauce stand, put a water pot on the stove. Once it’s boiling add salt and oil, then the pasta. This keeps the pasta from sticking and gives it a little bit of a taste.
  7. Check after 9-10 minutes to see if it’s ready, if you like it softer then wait longer.
  8. Heat up the sauce if needed.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
