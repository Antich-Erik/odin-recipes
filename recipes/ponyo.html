Ponyo's Ramen Noodles

Thin slices of bright pink ham, tasty egg and scallions

or us, ramen is a dish best served….. literally anytime, with anything. But, there’s just something especially tantalizing about the ramen shown in Ponyo

Luckily, this Ponyo ramen is one super easy dish to recreate from home.

Ingredients

  • 1 Package Nissin Chicken Ramen
  • 400ml Boiling water
  • 1 Slice cooked ham (cut in half) *use thick one if available
  • 1/2 Hard boiled egg
  • Chopped green onion

How to cook it?

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Place the noodles in a ramen bowl. Pour boiling water over the noodles and cover. Set and start your timer for three minutes
  3. After the first minute, quickly open, shake the noodles to separate, then arrange the ham, egg, and green onion over the noodles. Cover again and wait until the timer beeps.
  4. If you don't have a noodle bowl with a lid, you can cover the bowl with a plate!
  5. Enjoy!
